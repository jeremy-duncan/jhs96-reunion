About the Reunion

Thirty years ago, we crossed that stage in caps and gowns and headed out into the world. Now it's time to come back, share our stories, and celebrate the Class of 1996 — one of the greatest classes Judson ever produced. The Red Campus may be gone, but our memories live on.

Get Involved

RSVP so we know you're coming, post a class update to share what you've been up to, upload your old photos to the gallery, and spread the word to classmates you're still in touch with. Let's make this a reunion worth the 30-year wait!

The Red Campus

Our beloved Red Campus was razed in 2011 to make way for new athletic fields. But for the Class of '96 — the juniors and seniors who called it home — those red brick hallways are unforgettable. Share your Red Campus photos in our gallery to keep those memories alive.

Questions?

Reach out to the reunion committee at staff@judson96.org. We'll be updating this site as plans come together, so check back often for new details on events, activities, and logistics.
